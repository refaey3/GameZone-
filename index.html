Gallery

Spiderman

"With great power comes great responsibility." Peter Parker, an ordinary teenager turned superhero, swings across New York City, using his spider-like abilities to fight crime. Gifted with enhanced strength, agility, and a "spider-sense" that warns him of danger, he battles villains while juggling school, relationships, and his personal struggles. Despite his hardships, Spider-Man never gives up on protecting the innocent.

SuperMan

The last son of Krypton, Kal-El was sent to Earth as a baby and raised as Clark Kent. Under Earth's yellow sun, he developed godlike abilities—super strength, flight, heat vision, and near-invincibility. But more than his powers, it's his heart that makes him the world's greatest hero. Superman stands as a symbol of hope,

Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist, but when anger takes over, he transforms into the unstoppable Hulk. With immense strength and near-invulnerability, Hulk is both a hero and a force of destruction. While Banner seeks a cure, the world fears and respects the green giant. Whether smashing enemies or protecting those in need, Hulk remains one of the most powerful beings on Earth.

IronMan

Billionaire genius Tony Stark built his first Iron Man suit to escape captivity, but it became much more than just armor—it became a symbol of justice. With advanced technology, artificial intelligence, and a sharp wit, Stark fights to keep the world safe. Though once an arrogant playboy, he evolves into a true hero, proving that intelligence and determination can change the world.

Thor

As the mighty God of Thunder and the prince of Asgard, Thor wields the enchanted hammer, Mjolnir, to protect the realms. His strength rivals the greatest warriors, and his control over lightning makes him a formidable force. While once reckless, Thor has grown into a wise and noble hero, fighting to defend both Asgard and Earth from cosmic threats

Batman

Gotham City's Dark Knight, Bruce Wayne, witnessed his parents' murder as a child and vowed to rid the city of crime. With no superpowers, he relies on his intelligence, martial arts skills, and cutting-edge technology to strike fear into criminals. Batman is a master detective and strategist, always staying one step ahead of his enemies. His mission: to ensure no one else suffers the same pain he did
